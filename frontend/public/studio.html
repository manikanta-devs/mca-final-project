Set up your interview

Studio uses your Gemini API key to run a live, adaptive interview: it asks questions, listens to your spoken answers, follows up like a real interviewer, and gives you a scored review at the end. Your key and recordings never leave your browser — everything runs client-side.

Get one free at aistudio.google.com/apikey. Stored only in memory for this session.
Works best in Chrome or Edge (uses on-device speech recognition). Allow camera and microphone access when prompted.
HOW IT WORKS
  • Interviewer asks a question out loud
  • You answer on camera, in your own time
  • It naturally follows up, like a real panel
  • Moves through your set number of topics
  • Ends with a scored, written debrief
  • Download your answer clips + report
